Is there a free trial available?

Yes. You can start from the Free Starter plan, then upgrade when your team needs more users and deeper reporting.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can move between plans as your production volume grows, while keeping data continuity intact.

How does billing and cancellation work?

Billing can be monthly or annual based on your selected cycle. You can request cancellation before the next cycle starts.

Yes. Our team can assist setup, process mapping, and onboarding for sales, production, warehouse, and finance workflows.
